# STEP 3.5 — Institutions Review

## Purpose

Check whether the saved institutions baseline is concrete enough for later movement logic and twin-packaging work.

## Read inputs from

- `<STORY_ROOT>/state/world_baselines/WORLD_INSTITUTIONS.md`
- `<STORY_ROOT>/state/world_baselines/WORLD_RULES.md`
- `<STORY_ROOT>/state/world_baselines/WORLD_GEOGRAPHY.md`

## Save output to

- `<STORY_ROOT>/state/world_baselines/WORLD_INSTITUTIONS_REVIEW.md`

## Skill use

- No skill required for this step.

## Task

Review the saved institutions baseline against the acceptance criteria below and write one short review result.

## Acceptance criteria

### INSTITUTIONS

Pass when the major institutions are explicit, each institution has a practical role in ordinary life, and later steps could use the institutions without guessing who organizes or controls what.

### FACTIONS

Pass when the major factions are distinct, faction interests are legible, and faction influence appears in daily life instead of staying decorative.

### DAILY ORDER

Pass when the world has a readable normal-day structure, ordinary work, service, trade, duty, or supervision can be inferred from the written baseline, and later steps could place routines and conflicts inside a believable daily order.

### COMMON PRESSURES

Pass when recurring pressures are concrete, the baseline shows who feels those pressures most, and the pressures help explain why the world keeps functioning from day to day.

## Required output

### REVIEW RESULT

Write `STEP 3.5 PASS` or `STEP 3.5 FAIL`.

### BLOCKER

If the result is `FAIL`, state the blocker clearly in one sentence. If the result is `PASS`, write `None`.
