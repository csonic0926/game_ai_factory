# STEP 3 — Day Spine

## Purpose

Shape the selected story line into one chapter-day spine and save it as the chapter source’s day-spine artifact.

## Read inputs from

Read:

- `<STORY_ROOT>/state/chapter_sources/<ARTIFACT_STEM>_STORY_LINE.md`

## Save output to

Write:

- `<STORY_ROOT>/state/chapter_sources/<ARTIFACT_STEM>_DAY_SPINE.md`

## Skill use

- No skill required for this step.

## Task

Turn the selected story line into a one-day chapter spine.

Build the spine so it states:

- how the line begins in the morning
- how the line bends the day by noon
- how the day avoids a clean return to normal by evening
- what concrete thing carries from one time span into the next
- what the player carries out of the day

Use concrete supports from the selected line and the preflight, such as:

- locations
- recurring NPCs
- objects
- routines
- institutions
- pressure patterns
- time-flow facts

## Required output blocks

Always include:

- `MORNING`
- `NOON`
- `EVENING`
- `THROUGHLINE`

## Block definitions

### `MORNING`

State:

- starting situation
- intended task or obligation
- first visible bend

### `NOON`

State:

- what the player is still trying to finish
- what redirects, delays, or complicates that effort
- what concrete thing carries into evening

### `EVENING`

State:

- what should have been normal closure
- what now blocks that closure
- what end-of-day posture remains

### `THROUGHLINE`

State:

- the day’s original track
- the bending line across the day
- the consequence carried out of the day
